Our Story

EduLink was founded in 2010 by Ms. Priyadarshani Silva, a passionate educator with over 20 years of experience. Her vision was to create a learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. Starting with a small group of students, EduLink has grown into a leading tuition center in Sri Lanka, known for its innovative teaching methods and personalized approach to education.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in their academic pursuits and beyond.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student has access to high-quality education that caters to their individual needs and learning styles.

Our Approach

At EduLink, we believe in a holistic approach to education. Our methodology combines interactive lectures, collaborative projects, and personalized tutoring to ensure that students not only grasp the subject matter but also develop essential skills such as problem-solving, communication, and teamwork.

Achievements & Accreditations

EduLink has received numerous accolades, including the 'Best Tuition Center' award in 2018 and 2020. Our students consistently achieve top scores in national exams. We are also accredited by the National Education Board, ensuring our programs meet the highest standards.
