Dr. Rohan Perera

Mathematics Instructor

Teaching since 2010

Biography

Dr. Rohan Perera is a seasoned mathematics instructor with over 14 years of experience in teaching at the university level. He holds a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Colombo and specializes in calculus, linear algebra, and differential equations. Dr. Perera is known for his engaging teaching style and his ability to make complex mathematical concepts accessible to students of all levels.

Teaching Philosophy

Dr. Perera believes in fostering a student-centered learning environment where students are encouraged to actively participate in the learning process. He emphasizes problem-solving skills and critical thinking, aiming to equip students with the tools they need to succeed not only in mathematics but also in their future careers.
